# Slice 4: Loyalty system

> Detailed scope to be filled in after Slice 3 ships.

## Goal

Reward members for consistency and re-engagement. Behavior-based, not points-based.

## Likely scope

- Streak tracking (e.g. 3+ visits per week for N consecutive weeks)
- Milestone rewards (50, 100, 250 visits)
- Comeback incentives (member who returns after a gap)
- `rewards` table design (deferred from data model)
- Member-side: view earned rewards
- Admin-side: define reward rules per gym

## Open questions

- Are rewards purely cosmetic (badges) or do they have real value (free month, merch)?
- Are reward rules per-gym or platform-wide defaults?
- How are rewards redeemed — by admin marking them used?
